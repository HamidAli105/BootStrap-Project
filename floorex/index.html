Our Guarantee
FROM START TO FINISH

It’s our mission to ensure you feel protected at every stage of the project. To achieve this our Complete Customer Care system makes sure that your new floor is fully guaranteed. If one of our floors fails under day to day use we’re prepared to pull it up and start again. Before, during and after your project, we’ve nailed down the hassle so you don’t have to worry.
